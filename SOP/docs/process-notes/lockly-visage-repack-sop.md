## Repack SOP Steps (from video observation)

### Phase 1: Unbox & Lay Out (0:00–0:10)
- Open shipping carton and retail box
- Lay out ALL components on clean workbench
- Separate into groups: lock body parts, accessories/hardware, documentation

### Phase 2: Accessory Verification (0:10–0:50)
- Check each bagged accessory against the **box contents diagram** printed on the blue accessory tray
- Verify: Wi-Fi Door Sensor Kit (sealed), touch screen cover, USB cable, all hardware bags, keys
- Inspect bags for seal integrity — components should be in original sealed packaging
- Count hardware bags against expected quantity
- **Estimated time: ~40 seconds**

### Phase 3: Accessory Tray Repack (0:50–1:10)
- Place verified accessories into the **blue accessory tray/box insert** in correct positions matching the printed layout
- Each accessory has a designated slot in the tray
- Fold/close the accessory tray
- **Estimated time: ~20 seconds**

### Phase 4: Retail Box Assembly (1:10–1:40)
- Place **foam insert** into the bottom of the retail box (charcoal/black foam with cutouts)
- Place the **accessory tray** into the box on top of foam
- Place the **interior assembly** (wrapped in tissue/bag) into its foam cutout — right side
- Place the **exterior lock body** (wrapped in tissue/bag) into its foam cutout — left side
- Place the **strike plate sub-box** alongside the lock components
- Tuck the **Activation Card** into the box (visible from top)
- **Estimated time: ~30 seconds**

### Phase 5: Documentation & Close (1:40–2:10)
- Place instruction manual and support cards on top of components
- Slide the "We're here to help / No need to return" card under the inner lid flap
- Close the retail box lid — verify "Visage" branding faces correct direction
- **Estimated time: ~30 seconds**

### Phase 6: Shipping Prep (2:10–2:37)
- Wrap retail box in protective plastic bag/sleeve
- Place repacked retail box into brown Lockly-branded shipping carton
- Verify shipping label matches SKU (PGK728WRHKMB)
- Close and seal shipping carton
- **Estimated time: ~27 seconds**

## Key Observations

1. **No functional testing** — This is a repack-only SOP (NTF/sellable condition). No battery test, no firmware reset, no motor cycling.
2. **Accessory completeness is the critical check** — The blue tray with printed layout serves as the verification tool. Missing accessories = route to different disposition.
3. **Component protection matters** — Lock bodies wrapped in tissue/bags, foam cutouts prevent cosmetic damage. This is a premium product (~$300+ retail).
4. **Activation Card handling** — Each unit has a unique activation card with QR code. This is product-specific and must stay matched to the unit.
5. **Shipping label has full SKU info** — PGK728WRHKMB includes color/finish variant data.
